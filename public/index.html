New to fuse beads? Three steps.

1

Place

Follow the numbered pattern and place beads on a pegboard, color by color.

2

Iron

Cover with ironing paper and iron on medium heat for ~20 seconds until beads fuse.

3

Peel & flip

Let it cool under a book, peel off, iron the other side. Done — display it!

Starter gear (if you have none)

  • Clear pegboard 29×29 — the size our patterns default to
  • Ironing paper — reusable, 4–5 sheets last months
  • Bead tweezers — optional but saves your fingertips
  • Any household iron works — no special tools needed
